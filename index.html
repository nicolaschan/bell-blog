About bell.plus

Bell is a synchronized timer that counts down to the next time the bell rings at school. This software continues to be developed by Los Altos High School alumni, nicolaschan and noloerino, who began working on it during their senior year of high school. The LAHS Hack Club helps to maintain the schedules for LAHS. Bell is an open-source project. The source code is available on our GitHub repository Chrome extension originally developed by noloerino.
